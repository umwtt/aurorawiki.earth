Geomagnetic Storms

Satellite Systems:

Satellite systems are an indispensable part of modern life. Geomagnetic storms can cause disruptions and interruptions in satellite signals, causing problems in many areas such as communications, navigation and weather forecasts. This may lead to disruption of air traffic, delays in maritime transport and disruptions in energy networks, leading to economic losses.
