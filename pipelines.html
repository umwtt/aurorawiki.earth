Geomagnetic Storms

Pipelines:

Electric currents caused by geomagnetic storms can accelerate corrosion in underground pipelines, causing serious leaks. This situation poses a great risk, especially for oil and natural gas pipelines. While oil spills harm marine life and coastal ecosystems, natural gas leaks can lead to explosions and fires, causing large-scale environmental disasters. Additionally, such leaks can lead to contamination of water resources and problems with drinking water supply.
