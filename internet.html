Geomagnetic Storms

Internet:

The Internet has become an indispensable part of modern life. Geomagnetic storms can cause disruptions in satellite and cable systems, leading to internet connection slowdowns, outages, and security vulnerabilities. This situation can cause economic losses by causing disruptions in many areas such as e-commerce, financial transactions, remote working and education.
